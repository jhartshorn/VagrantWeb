About Hops

Established in 1984, Hops provide keg beer to pubs, clubs, restaurants and hotels. We also provide an emergency out of hours service. We provide outside catering and bar services for any occasion. All our staff are friendly and reliable and we have an excellent reputation.

Our order line is open 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, we can supply kegs when you need them the most. We supply kegs from all major UK breweries and distributors.

We cover Derby and Derbyshire, Nottingham and Nottinghamshire, Leicester and Leicestershire, Burton and East Staffordshire. Other locations may be available on request.

Our Brands

We stock all the leading brands of keg lager, cider and bitter, including: Carling, Fosters, Carlsberg, Stella Artois, Grolsch, Coors Light, John Smiths, Bass, Worthington, Guinness.
